What We Offer

TESLA-SPECIFIC SERVICES

Panoramic Roof Tint
Tesla's massive glass roofs are stunning — and brutal in the Bakersfield heat. Our ceramic window film cuts solar heat significantly while keeping the view crystal clear.
Full-Front PPF
Tesla's frunk hood, front bumper, and fenders are highway rock-chip targets. Our computer-cut XPEL PPF gives them invisible armor that self-heals minor scratches.
Ceramic Coating
Protect Tesla's thin factory paint with a nano-ceramic shield that beads water, resists contaminants, and keeps the gloss looking fresh for years without constant upkeep.
Full-Body PPF Wrap
Complete bumper-to-bumper paint protection for the ultimate in coverage. Every panel protected, invisible, and backed by a manufacturer's warranty.
Interior Tint
Protect your Tesla's interior and passengers from UV exposure. Reduce fading in the cabin and stay cooler on Bakersfield's hot days.
Combo Packages
Tint + PPF + Ceramic together — the complete protection stack. Ask about our bundled Tesla packages for the best value.
