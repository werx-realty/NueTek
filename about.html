Who We Are

OUR STORY

NeuTEK Customs is a family business, founded and operated by two brothers who turned a lifelong passion for vehicles into Bakersfield's most trusted auto protection shop.

What started as a shared obsession with keeping cars looking their best has grown into a full-service studio equipped with the industry's finest materials. As an XPEL Authorized Dealer, we bring manufacturer-backed products and precision installation techniques to every single job.

Our appointment-only model is intentional — it means your vehicle always gets our complete, undivided focus. No rush jobs. No shortcuts. Just results that speak for themselves.

We're proud to serve the Bakersfield community and beyond, and honored to be the preferred tint shop of Tesla of Bakersfield.
